What is Acceptance Testing?

Acceptance testing is the final test to see if a product works for real users. It’s like checking a pizza before you eat it: is it cooked, and does it have the right toppings?

Simple Interactive Example

You are the user. Choose if the product works or not, and decide if it should be accepted or rejected.
